Description

The Alchemist" is a novel written by Paulo Coelho. It is a philosophical and inspiring book that tells the story of a shepherd boy named Santiago who embarks on a journey to fulfill his personal legend, which is his destiny or life's purpose. The book is a blend of adventure, fantasy, and spiritualism, and it is widely regarded as a modern classic. It has sold millions of copies worldwide and has been translated into numerous languages.
