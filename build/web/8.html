Details of Book

Author :  Ashok Kumawat

Price :  105

Description

A story of struggle is a motivational fiction book that embarks to motivate readers for success. The story describes the life struggle of a teenager boy. A 17-year-old boy belongs to a poor family and falls in love with a beautiful girl. But the girl's father wants her to marry a rich boy. Therefore, the boy leaves his village and goes to the city to become rich and this is where his struggle starts. A Story of Struggle is an inspiring storybook that inspires readers to succeed.
