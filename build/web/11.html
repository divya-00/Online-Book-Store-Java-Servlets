Details of Book

Author :  Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Price :  199

Description

"Wings of Fire" is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India. The book was first published in 1999 and co-authored by Arun Tiwari.The book tells the story of Kalam's life, from his humble beginnings growing up in a small village in Tamil Nadu to his rise as a leading scientist and statesman. Kalam shares his experiences working on India's space and missile programs, including his work on the successful Pokhran-II nuclear tests. Kalam's passion for science is a recurring theme throughout book.
