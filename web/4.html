Description

The story of Snow White is a fairy tale that has been retold and adapted by many authors throughout history. However, the most well-known version of Snow White was published by the Brothers Grimm in 1812 in their collection of fairy tales, "Grimms' Fairy Tales." The story of Snow White is known for its themes of jealousy, beauty, and the power of true love. It has inspired countless adaptations, and other retellings, cementing Snow White's place as one of the most beloved fairy tales of all time.
